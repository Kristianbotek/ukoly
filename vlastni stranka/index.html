About

Botek-Bytes was created by me, Kristián Botek, a 16 year old student from Czech Republic, who is studying programming at school in his home town. At this time the company is only made of me, so our production will not be as fast as other's but I will put my sweat, blood and tears into your dream website and that's what makes Botek-Bytes special!

For now in Botek-Bytes we develop websites in HTML, CSS and soon in JavaScript. Now our main goal is to get perfect at CSS and then start coding in JavaScript, many website developing companies think only the functionality of the website matters, in Botek-Bytes we think the looks of the website matter as much as the functionality, with our creative minds your project will be unique and beautiful!

As mentioned, our development is quite slower than others but it is worth the wait! And we hope that in the future the wait time will be a lot lower!
